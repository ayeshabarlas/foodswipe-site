FoodSwipe – App Information & Policies

About FoodSwipe

FoodSwipe is a mobile application-based food ordering and delivery service. Users can browse restaurants, place orders, and make digital payments securely. All payments are processed via PayFast.

Our Services

  • Online Food Ordering
  • Restaurant Listings
  • Order Tracking
  • Digital Payments
  • Rider Allocation
  • Order History
  • Customer Support
  • Discounts & Promotions

Privacy Policy

FoodSwipe collects user information only for order processing, service delivery, and application improvement. User data is never sold or shared with third parties, except trusted payment partners such as PayFast.

Return & Refund Policy

As FoodSwipe provides digital food delivery services, refunds are only applicable in cases of failed transactions, duplicate payments, or technical issues. Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days.

Shipping / Service Policy

FoodSwipe facilitates food delivery services through partner restaurants and riders. Service availability depends on restaurant operating hours and location.

Terms & Conditions

By using FoodSwipe, users agree to comply with all applicable terms. Any misuse of the platform is prohibited. FoodSwipe is not responsible for delays caused by restaurants or riders. All payments are securely handled via PayFast.
